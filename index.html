    Bale Pratama

    Architecture

    Type: Residential
    Location: Ciputat, Tangerang
    Year: 2012

    MIX OF MINIMALIST

    The concept is minimalist architecture with essential quality and achieve simplicity. Focus on how to make more with less.

    Designed it to make people live in a spacious home comfortably. Mix of concrete and wood material and add monochromatic colors to inject premium look.

    Jatipadang House

    Architecture

    Type: Residential
    Location: Perum, Karang Pola
    Year: 2014

    A Space of Serenity

    Jatipadang House is the true reflection of the saying ‘home is where the heart is'. Built for retired people, wanted cozy, comfortable, and with plenty of green scenery around the house, Jatipadang House is truly a space of serenity.

    Shelter for big family to gather around their beloved people, we provided one big space in the fist floor with warm homey feeling. Outdoor pool for the kids to enjoy.

    LOT 36

    Architecture

    Type: Commercial Store
    Location: Panglima Polim, Jakarta Selatan
    Year: 2012

    Brick and Mortar Store

    A one stop shopping hub located at the heart of South Jakarta, LOT 36 is inspired by the emerging culture of its host town. Adopting the vintage looks with rustic and industrial theme, LOT 36 is brick-and-mortar store in the most literal sense.

    With spacious display room, large windows and vivid lighting, LOT 36 is specifically designed to bring the most memorable shopping experience for customers.

    Meranti House

    Architecture

    Type: Residential
    Location: Semarang, Jawa Tengah
    Year: 2012

    A Traditional Accent

    A blend between traditional and contemporary looks, Meranti House as the name suggests, is built with heavy accent of Meranti wood, a native wood of Southeast Asia with distinctive dark brown color.

    The contrast between the wood material with gray concrete resulting in a unique appearance. Meranti House is not only pleasing to look at, the house is a comfortable, spacious home that enables the owner to engage in both leisure and productive activities.

    WIDA House I

    Architecture

    Type: Residential
    Location: Jatiwaringin, Jakarta Timur
    Year: 2014

    Light and Refreshing

    Injecting the Industrial theme with Masculine accent to a home, to satisfied the owner passion to big motorcycle. We provided special access towards the backyard where the fun begin, a bar with pool table.

    Still it is a residential space with contemporary design sits well with its surrounding urban environment.

    With plenty sources of natural lighting and air circulation, the house is as refreshing as it is well-lit and bright.

    PT. Danareksa Investment Management

    Interior

    Type: Commercial Office
    Location: Jakarta Pusat
    Year: 2015

    Team ís Workspace

    “Talent wins games, but teamwork and intelligence wins championships.” - Michael Jordan

    Inspired by the legend’s say, we put those spirit into the workspace design. We transform a closed, cramped office into an open office. Tearing down boundaries while increasing productivity and communication, a recipe for successful teamwork.

    Dispelling cubicle and adding a touch in the furniture and lighting creates a cozier workspace but still with a serious mood.

    Eat 'n Treat

    Interior

    Type: Commercial Office
    Location: Pluit, Jakarta Utara
    Year: 2014

    Treat For Your Senses

    Food are made to indulge human’s taste senses. Eat n Treat fully understand that and that’s what they deliver. But we make it a step further… and better.

    With premium look crafted by using wood element and cozy ambience produced by the lighting, Eat n Treat promises to treat your sight from Jakarta’s intricacy.

    Extensive use of glass in many aspect of the design, like the food display, served as focal points and able creates a spacious impression even in a small space.

    Home Library

    Interior

    Type: Residential
    Location: Pondok Labu, Jakarta Selatan
    Year: 2014

    Re-purpose unused attic into gentleman cave

    Every man need a place of his own, which he can collect his own thought while doing what he loves doing. You all know it as a man’s cave. Exactly what we were try to transform. The owner always dream a space of his own passion.

    We simply design a library / work space type of man’s cave. He professionally loves teaching and book is his vacaEon. He also like to collect acEon figures, which perfect to add masculine touch. Therefore we built a shelf haven for his book and acEon figure collecEon. We put his anEque furniture to juxtapose with our modern sense of lighEng and TV cabinet.

    Finally a touch of homey nuance of couch and paint color just to inject relax feel.

    INACO Cooking Class

    Interior

    Type: Commercial Office
    Location: Tambun, Bekasi TImur
    Year: 2014

    A Classroom in Factory

    Inaco cooking class is one of our design that we can proudly say unique. It’s a combination of 3 worlds and style, Inaco’s sophisticated factory, a kitchen, and a classroom

    By Combining Brick usage on the wall with wood aspects on the panels and state of the art tools, We merge a productivity of factory, creativity of kitchen, and knowledge of a classroom mindset into the design.

    This contradictive and unusual design offers a special experience to everyone happened to be in this room.

    KEMENTERIAN PEKERJAAN UMUM

    Interior

    Type: Governmental Office
    Location: Blok M, Jakarta Selatan
    Year: 2015

    TRANSFORMING WORK

    Upon hearing the word ‘office’, most people would automatically form a mental image of rigid, boring, conventional space – doubly so for government office.

    Our project for Kementerian Pekerjaan Umum defies those expectations, with heavy uses of wood and thoughtful use of space, we transform office into second home.

    Takigawa Restaurant

    Interior

    Type: Commercial Restaurant
    Location: Cilandak Town Square, Jakarta Selatan
    Year: 2014

    Essence of Origami

    The importance of ORIGAMI is its indentation. We applied it in the ceiling and chairs. Once you are inside and sit you will feel like you are in a paper folded space eating Japanese food. Quite the theme isn’t it.

    Since its Japanese it feels right just to add another Japanese attribute, such as nature nuance and woody feeling which we applied in the color tone and material.

    We use lots of mirrors to make it feel spacious. Even though it’s crowded, you will still experience a wide and quite a big space around you.
