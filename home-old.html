LET'S START SOMETHING.

Once upon a time at the end of 2013, Dita, Esa, and Reyhan meet up to hangout at LUCY IN THE SKY. This is the day when Cipeng (Reyhan nickname) came up with a random question:

"Why don't we start our own architecture and interior design firm?"

This question become the seed of an idea of IN-SCALE.

LET'S MAKE A DIFFERENCE.

Two months after, at Dita's Apartment the three of them regroup try to form a concept. Esa & Reyhan speak up their mind about the situation of the architecture and interior design industry. Mainly about the monotonous and boring design that they observe. With their stories, came a bold decision...

"WE NEED TO MAKE A CHANGE!"

SPACE TO WORK

Realize they will need an office, they try to find the perfect place. Wanted a space where they can work but still feel at home. Surveyed several places, but none of them like it.

"Too rigid! Too office like! Too expensive! Too homey! Too... just not right!"

SERENDIPITY SOMETIMES LEAD TO THE RIGHT WAY.

After several weeks trying to found the perfect space, finally they found it. Not by searching, asking, or calling people. . . but they find it serendipitously. While Dita strolling around, she found a nice looking cluster and decided to check it out. Good neighborhood, homey place, yet the atmosphere is nice to be an office. Finally they get their office/home. . .

While finishing the legal administration for their newly found business, they realize it need a name soon. Esa came up with a name "In-Space" (which later on become the name of one project). But somehow in doesn't feel right, then Reyhan tweak it into “IN-SCALE”.
