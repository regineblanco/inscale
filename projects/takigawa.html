Essence of Origami

The importance of ORIGAMI is its indentation. We applied it in the ceiling and chairs. Once you are inside and sit you will feel like you are in a paper folded space eating Japanese food. Quite the theme isn’t it.

Since its Japanese it feels right just to add another Japanese attribute, such as nature nuance and woody feeling which we applied in the color tone and material.

We use lots of mirrors to make it feel spacious. Even though it’s crowded, you will still experience a wide and quite a big space around you.
